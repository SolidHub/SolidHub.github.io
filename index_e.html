Charity Foundation «Charity school “Smart Future”» - is the first in Ukraine Charity school for personal and IT development of children.

We empower for vulnerable children to learn about network and cloud technologies, software development, robot programming, and to make first steps in the development of his career earlier than most peers.

Educational programms of Charity school “Smart Future” Designed specifically for both junior and senior pupils. The programs introduce children to modern technologies, to life in the modern information world and the variety of possibilities in it.

All programs have a practical focus and are preparing for future work in the IT industry.


Our mission is to fulfill children's dreams and to give a happy future.



The main purpose of the Foundation is to provide quality education and personal development to children and adolescents of socially disadvantaged groups:


  • orphans
  • disabled children
  • children from large families (having 5 or more children)
  • children of military personnel who died or were injured are incompatible with work

For this purpose Charity Foundation «Charity school “Smart Future”» raises funds from philanthropists, organizes the educational process, develops and implements training programs. We focus our efforts on learning modern technology because the IT industry is the most stable, most profitable and the most promising of all industries.

Activity results



The Charity Foundation was founded in September 2016. During this time more than 1000 children from different IT fields were trained. Also, more than 400 children have learned IT English.


With the help of partners and donors of the Foundation, training, excursions to IT companies, internships, IT camps were organized and conducted.



The training takes place in the premises of the Foundation, the training programs are provided by a partner of the Foundation - ІТ school Main School.
