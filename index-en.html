CHARITY FUND

"SMART FUTURE"

❮ ❯



Charity Fund «Charity school “Smart Future”» - is the first in Ukraine Charity school for personal and IT development of children.

We empower vulnerable children to learn about network and cloud technologies, software development, robot programming, and first steps in the development of his career earlier than most peers.

The Charity school “Smart Future” curriculum is designed specifically for students of both junior and senior grades. The programs introduce children to modern technology, life in the modern information world and the variety of opportunities in it.

All programs have a practical focus and are preparing for future work in the IT industry.


Our mission is to fulfill childhood dreams and to give a happy future.


The main purpose of the Foundation is to provide quality education and personal development to children and adolescents of socially disadvantaged groups:


  • orphans
  • children with disabilities
  • children from large families (having 5 or more children)
  • children of military personnel who died or were injured are incompatible with work

For that purpose, the Charity Fund «Charity school “Smart Future”» is raising funds from philanthropists, organizes the educational process, develops and implements training programs. We have focused our efforts on training in modern technology, because the IT industry is the most stable, most profitable and the most promising of all industries.

Results of activity


The Charity Fund was founded in September 2016. During this time more than 1000 children from different IT fields were trained. Also, more than 400 children have learned IT English.


With the help of partners and donors of the Foundation, training, excursions to IT companies, internships, IT camps were organized and conducted.



Training takes place at the premises of the Foundation, the training programs are provided by a partner of the Fund - ІТ school Main School.
