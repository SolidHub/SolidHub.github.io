Charity Fund
"Smart Future"

Our mission is to fulfill childhood dreams



Charity Fund «Charity School
“Smart Future” »

is the first in Ukraine Charity school for personal and IT development of children. We empower vulnerable children to learn about network and cloud technologies, software development, robot programming, and first steps in the development of his career earlier than most peers.

The Charity school “Smart Future” curriculum is designed specifically for students of both junior and senior grades. The programs introduce children to modern technology, life in the modern information world and the variety of opportunities in it. All programs have a practical focus and are preparing for future work in the IT industry.


Our mission is to fulfill childhood dreams and to give a happy future.



The main purpose of the Foundation is to provide quality education and personal development to children and adolescents of socially disadvantaged groups:


  • orphans
  • children with disabilities
  • children from large families (having 5 or more children)
  • children of military personnel who died or were injured are incompatible with work

For that purpose, the Charity Fund «Charity school “Smart Future”» is raising funds from philanthropists, organizes the educational process, develops and implements training programs. We have focused our efforts on training in modern technology, because the IT industry is the most stable, most profitable and the most promising of all industries.


Forms and methods of program implementation: classroom, lab work, training, field trips, workshops, motivational speeches, creative projects and tasks, discussions, brainstorming, games, homework, internships and more.


We invite philanthropists to support our projects and help children
take the first steps in IT and get a quality IT education, and possibly their first job.

Results of activity




The Charity Foundation was founded in September 2016. During this time more training was conducted than 1000 children from different IT fields. Also, more than 400 children have learned IT English.

The training is held at the premises of the Foundation, the training programs are provided by the partner of the Foundation - Main School IT School.



With the help of partners and donors of the Foundation, training, excursions to IT companies, internships, IT camps were organized and conducted.
