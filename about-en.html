Charity Fund «Charity School “Smart future” »


Charity Fund “Smart Future” is the first in Ukraine charity school for children's personal and IT Development. We empower children in vulnerable communities to learn about network and cloud technologies, software development, robot programming, and take the first steps in developing your own careers before most peers.

The Charity School curriculum is designed specifically for students of both junior and senior grades. The programs introduce children to modern technologies, to life in the modern information world and the variety of opportunities in it. All programs have a practical focus and are preparing for future work in the IT industry.

The main purpose of the Fund is to provide quality education and personal development to children and adolescents of socially disadvantaged groups:

  • orphans
  • children with disabilities
  • children from large families (having 5 or more children)
  • children of military personnel who were killed or injured are incompatible with their work

To this end, the Foundation for a Charitable School “A Smart Future” is raising funds from philanthropists, organizes the educational process, develops and implements training programs. We have focused our efforts training in modern technology, because the IT industry is the most stable, most profitable and the most promising of all industries.

Forms and methods of implementation of the program: classroom, laboratory work, trainings, excursions, masterclasses, motivational speeches, creative projects and tasks, discussions, brainstorming, games, homework, internships and more.

We invite philanthropists to support our projects and help children
take the first steps in IT and get a quality IT education, and possibly their first job.

Activity results


The Charity Fund was founded in September 2016. During this time more than 1000 children were training from different IT fields. Also, more than 400 children have learned IT English.

The training is held at the classes of the Fund, the training programs are provided by the partner of the Fund - IT School Main School .


With the help of partners and donors of the Fund, training, excursions to IT companies, internships, IT camps were organized and conducted.
