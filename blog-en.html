Charity school "Smart Future"

During the New Year, you want miracles and surprises, especially for children. That is why the Charitable School "Smart Future" together with their wonderful partner, Svitla Systems, launched a 2D animation course in November and gave children the opportunity to create their own Christmas story. Every week, step by step, the children put their ideas and dreams into action. On December 24, their projects were defended, and the benefactors with festive moods and Christmas gifts came to visit. After protecting children's projects, I expected a sweet table and a sea of ​​positive impressions. We thank ICF "Life with Excess" for pleasant gifts and the Kyiv City Center for the Rehabilitation of Children with Disabilities for many years of cooperation.
